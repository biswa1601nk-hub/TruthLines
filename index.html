How TruthLine Works

Our decentralized verification process ensures transparency and accuracy in fighting misinformation

1

Users submit suspicious news, images, or claims that need verification

2

3

Vote

Community members vote on the veracity based on evidence

4

Verify

Claims are marked as verified true, false, or misleading
